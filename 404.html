About CompareText

CompareText is a professional, privacy-first text comparison tool that runs entirely in your browser. No servers, no accounts — your text never leaves your device.

Features

  • Multi-level comparison — Compare at line, word, or character granularity.
  • LCS-based diff engine — Professional longest common subsequence algorithm, not naive index comparison.
  • Flexible options — Ignore case, spaces, tabs, blank lines, punctuation, and more.
  • Three result views — Side-by-side, unified, and detailed statistics.
  • Syntax highlighting — Color-coded additions, deletions, modifications, and whitespace changes.
  • File support — Upload TXT, LOG, CSV, JSON, XML, HTML, CSS, and JS files.
  • Drag & drop — Drop files directly onto either editor panel.
  • Keyboard shortcuts — Compare, switch texts, and clear without touching the mouse.
  • Live compare — See results update as you type.
  • Dark mode — Easy on the eyes for long sessions.

Security

All text comparison happens locally in your browser using vanilla JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device. Google Analytics is used for anonymous site usage statistics only — it does not receive your compared text.

FAQ

Is CompareText free?
Yes, completely free with no limitations.
Do I need to create an account?
No. CompareText works instantly without registration.
Is my data stored anywhere?
Only in your browser's local storage (theme preference, settings, and last compared text). Nothing is sent to any server.
What diff algorithm does CompareText use?
CompareText uses the Longest Common Subsequence (LCS) algorithm with Myers-style optimization for character-level diffs, supporting moved line detection.
Can I compare code files?
Yes. Upload or paste JS, CSS, HTML, JSON, XML, CSV, LOG, and TXT files.
Does it work offline?
After the initial page load (and font cache), CompareText works fully offline.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not a feature — it's the foundation of CompareText.

Cookies

CompareText does not use cookies for functionality. Google Analytics may set cookies to measure anonymous site usage. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

Analytics

CompareText uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views, general traffic patterns, and how visitors navigate the site. This helps us understand usage and improve the tool.

Your compared text is never sent to Google or any server — all diff processing stays in your browser. See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles analytics data.

Other Third-Party Services

CompareText also loads the Inter font from Google Fonts. No advertising trackers or fingerprinting code are used.

No Login

There is no user account system. You never need to sign up, log in, or provide any personal information to use CompareText.

Everything Runs Locally

All text comparison, diff computation, file reading, and export operations happen entirely within your browser. No text, files, or comparison results are ever transmitted to any server.

Local Storage

CompareText uses your browser's localStorage to remember:

  • Your theme preference (light or dark)
  • Comparison settings (ignore case, live compare, etc.)
  • The last text you compared (so you can resume your work)

This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.
